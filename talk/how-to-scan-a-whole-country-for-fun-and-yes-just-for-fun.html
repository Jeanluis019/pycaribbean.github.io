David Tavarez

How to scan a whole country for fun and... yes, just for fun!


Audience: Intermediate


Description:

Python is one of the favorite programming languages used by Security Experts to develop the most advanced pentesting tools. In this talk we're going to audit most of the public IP blocks of the Dominican Republic to find popular remote vulnerabilities used by cyber criminals worldwide to gain unauthorized access. The main goal is to make ourselves aware of the danger of underestimating efforts in securing ours companies and clients.
