David Hemmat

Hiring top talent for your team


Audience: All


Description:

In this talk we will explore what things developers are looking for, and how to hire them and keep them on your team. We will be discussing the following topics: - Why developers look to change jobs - What they are looking for in a new job or project - How to interview well without getting false negatives - How to keep your dev team happy
