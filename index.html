PyCaribbean Special Dinner

The PyCaribbean Special Dinner is an event where you can share a good time with your fellow Python lover friends while enjoying some of the best typical food that the Dominican Republic has to offer.

The event will be held at the El Conuco, a restaurant known for it's great food and exhuberant atmosphere mixing traditional Dominican music and dancing with a top notch customer service. Better yet, it's at walking distance to "El Malecon", a historic center with a beautiful view, great for a night walk.

Buy Special Dinner Ticket

PyCaribbean Beach Trip

What a better way to end the conference than a beach trip to one of the most beautiful beaches of the Dominican Republic. Imagine traveling, eating, sharing and laughing for a full day with your fellow PyCaribbean attendees, this is what the PyCaribbean Beach Trip is all about!

We'll be going to Hotel Alisei in Las Terrenas, a nice place with good food, a relaxing atmosphene and great access to the beach. The trip includes transport to and from the hotel, breakfast, snacks and lunch at the hotel.
