Who is Ivan Torroledo?

Ivan Torroledo is a data scientist in the Cyxtera Research organization. In this role, he develops and implements machine and deep learning algorithms to enhance phishing detection, network security, fraud detection, and malware mitigation. Ivan is also highly interested in research on the application of machine and deep learning in high energy physics and astrophysics. Before joining Cyxtera, he worked at the Central Bank of Colombia, applying high performance computing tools to monetary policy analysis. He is passionate about applying the most advanced scientific knowledge to cyber security industry. Ivan holds degrees in Economics and Physics.
