Who is Jeremy Meiss?

Jeremy is the Head of Community at Auth0 and former Director of DevRel/Community at XDA-Developers. He is a lover of all things coffee, community, open source, and tech. He has a wife, 3 kids, and a beagle who all profess undying love.
