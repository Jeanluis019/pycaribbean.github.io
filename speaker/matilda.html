Who is Matilda?

Matilda is a trans disabled artist, community organizer, and programmer studying, imagining, and leveraging mathematical, ecological, and social systems for a more just, equitable, and inclusive world. They have organized around racial, disability, climate, and housing justice and are currently a Data Science fellow at The Knowledge House in New York. They are interested in p2p networks as a way of allowing communities to protect themselves in the face of hostile forces, be they social, economic, or ecological
