Who is David Hemmat?

David is an entrepreneur and developer based in the Dominican Republic. He currently works as CEO at Blue Coding and Resolve Digital. In his previous life he has done all sorts of development work for companies both large and small, and has focused a good amount of time and energy on Ruby on Rails. In his free time David likes to read about entrepreneurship and management, ride motorcycles and do brazilian jiu-jitsu.
