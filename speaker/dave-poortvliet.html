Who is Dave Poortvliet?

Dave has been providing technical direction and oversight of university web operations at Grand Valley State University for over 15 years. He ensures design and development of custom web applications align with the strategic goals of the organization. Dave leads, manages, and mentors a talented team of web developers, designers, and student workers to deliver custom web applications that provide value to the faculty, staff, and students of the university.
