Who is Louise Grandjonc?

I'm Louise, I work as a software engineer at Citus Data. Previously I was a lead python developer in a crowdfunding company.

I enjoy query tuning SQL, working on performances in general, writing python and C... I'm also active in the PostgreSQL community, created a woman in the Postgres group, organize meetups and conferences. I'm pretty passionate by PostgreSQL and distributed system, so please come meet me! I also like more human things, like playing music and baking.
